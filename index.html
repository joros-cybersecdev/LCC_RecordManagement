System Features

Centralized Academic Records System

Designed for students, teachers, and administrators of La Consolacion College.

Student Grade Viewing

Students can view their grades once their tuition is fully paid and the teacher has granted access.

Teacher Grade Management

Teachers can input and manage grades for their specific subjects and sections only.

Access Control

Teachers control which students can view their grades. Admin manages payment status per student.

Admin Management

Admin manages all student and teacher accounts, payment status, sections, and subjects.

Program Management

Supports BSHM, BSIT, and BSBA programs including BSBA majors in Financial and Business Management.

Secure Role-Based Access

Each user only sees what they are allowed to see. Students, teachers, and admins have separate dashboards.

About the System

Built for La Consolacion College

This Web-Based Records Management System was developed exclusively for the college department of La Consolacion College. It replaces traditional and manual methods of managing academic records with a centralized, secure, and efficient digital platform.

✓ Students view grades only after paying tuition
✓ Teachers manage grades for their own subjects only
✓ Admin controls payment status and all accounts
✓ Supports BSHM, BSIT, and BSBA programs
✓ Evaluated based on ISO 25010 quality standards
